This is the character Euria by artist Marcks on Deviantart. This character always has a paranoid and bleak veiw on the world so I wanted to capture that in his expression. He has the power to control fire so I wanted it to be demonstrated in this piece as he waves his arms to control the power of the flames. I chose a dark background color inorder to make the flames pop out to the viewer.
