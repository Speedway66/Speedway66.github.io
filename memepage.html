Here is a meme mashup of the Surprised Patrick meme and the Anakin Hates Sand meme. Alone these two memes can be used in a variety of ways for infinite fun but together they can create something new and unique together. Surprised Patrick's reaction adds a new humorous take on the sand meme as before I never found any material where Padme is given a reaction to Anakin's statement. Surprised Patrick gives her a reaction that serves a humorous response as well as a symbol for our own ironic thoughts.
