Here is a glitch art piece of former president Barack Obama. Taking the text of his inauguration address and inputting it into the coding of an image of the former president at his address presents us with this distorted image. This piece is suppose to demonstrate how words are just words and when you peal back the surface of of a presidents words you get distortion and nothing is clear anymore. What you see on the surface isn't what it seems when looking from a different angle.
