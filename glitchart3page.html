This is a glitch art piece of President Trump giving his inauguration address. This piece was created by splicing the audio of his inauguration speech into the coding of a picture of him at his inaugration to give this distorted image of the president. The creepy visual of this art piece represents the ominous feeling many people have about how Trump's presidency will shape the future of our country. Many people saw a dark future ahead as Trump was giving his speech. Now whether or not people still feel that way now is up to them.
